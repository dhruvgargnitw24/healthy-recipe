Boiled Egg Recipe

Calories

A single boiled egg contains approximately 70-80 calories, depending on its size.

Health Benefits

  1. High in Protein: A great source of high-quality protein for muscle building and repair.
  2. Rich in Healthy Fats: Contains omega-3 fatty acids that support heart health.
  3. Boosts Brain Function: Eggs are rich in choline, essential for brain function.
  4. Contains Essential Vitamins: Provides vitamin A, B12, D, and E.
  5. Supports Eye Health: Rich in lutein and zeaxanthin, antioxidants that promote eye health.
  6. Low in Calories: A perfect snack for those on a calorie-controlled diet.

Instructions to Make

  1. Ingredients:
    • Egg(s)
    • Water
    • Salt (optional)
  2. Instructions:
    1. Boil Water: Fill a pot with enough water to cover the eggs and bring it to a boil.
    2. Add Eggs: Gently add the eggs to the boiling water and reduce the heat to a simmer.
    3. Cook: Boil the eggs for 9-12 minutes, depending on how firm you like your yolks.
    4. Cool: Remove the eggs and place them in cold water for 5 minutes to cool down.
    5. Peel and Serve: Peel the eggs and enjoy them as a snack, or add salt and pepper to taste.
