Vegetable Pulao Recipe

Calories

A typical serving of vegetable pulao contains around 250-300 calories depending on the choice of vegetables and rice.

Health Benefits

  1. Rich in Fiber: Vegetables and whole grains provide fiber that aids digestion.
  2. Packed with Nutrients: Vegetables provide essential vitamins and minerals, supporting overall health.
  3. Heart-Healthy: The addition of whole grains like brown rice helps lower cholesterol levels.

Instructions to Make

  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 cup basmati rice
    • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 2-3 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
  2. Instructions:
    1. Sauté Spices: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Sauté until aromatic.
    2. Cook Vegetables: Add onions and cook until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
    3. Combine Rice: Add the rice and vegetables, and cook for 5 minutes.
    4. Cook Pulao: Add water, salt, and garam masala. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until cooked.
    5. Serve: Fluff the rice and serve with raita or salad.
