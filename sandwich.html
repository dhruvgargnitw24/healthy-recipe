Sandwich Recipe

Calories

A typical vegetable sandwich contains around 250-350 calories, depending on the ingredients and bread used.

Health Benefits

  1. Rich in Fiber: Helps with digestion and keeps you full for longer.
  2. High in Nutrients: Provides essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants from vegetables.
  3. Good Source of Protein: If you add cheese, legumes, or lean meats, sandwiches can be a good source of protein.
  4. Versatile: Sandwiches can be made with a variety of healthy ingredients like whole-grain bread, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  5. Low in Fat (when made with healthy options): Choose healthy spreads like avocado or low-fat cheese to make it heart-healthy.

Instructions to Make

  1. Ingredients:
    • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
    • 1/2 avocado (optional)
    • Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach, etc.)
    • Tomato slices
    • Cucumber slices
    • Cheese (optional)
    • Hummus or mustard (optional)
    • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  2. Instructions:
    1. Prepare Vegetables: Wash and slice the vegetables (tomatoes, cucumber, etc.).
    2. Spread: If using, spread hummus or mustard on one side of each bread slice.
    3. Assemble: Layer the bread with vegetables, avocado, and cheese.
    4. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste.
    5. Serve: Close the sandwich and cut it into halves or quarters. Serve immediately.
