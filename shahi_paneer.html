Shahi Paneer Recipe

Calories

A serving of Shahi Paneer contains approximately 300-350 calories, depending on the amount of cream or ghee used.

Health Benefits

  1. Rich in Protein: Paneer provides high-quality protein for muscle building and repair.
  2. Calcium-Rich: Paneer is an excellent source of calcium, important for bone health.
  3. Good for Heart Health: When cooked with minimal oil, Shahi Paneer can be a heart-healthy choice.
  4. Packed with Vitamins: Contains vitamin A, B12, and other essential vitamins for overall well-being.

Instructions to Make

  1. Ingredients:
    • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
    • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
    • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/4 cup cream or cashew paste
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
    • Salt to taste
  2. Instructions:
    1. Cook the Base: Heat ghee in a pan, add onions, and saut√© until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
    2. Prepare the Gravy: Add tomato puree, garam masala, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Cook until the oil separates.
    3. Add Paneer: Add paneer cubes and cream or cashew paste. Cook for 5-7 minutes until well coated.
    4. Serve: Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with naan or roti.
