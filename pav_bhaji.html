Pav Bhaji Recipe

Calories

A serving of Pav Bhaji contains approximately 350-400 calories, depending on the amount of butter used.

Health Benefits

  1. High in Fiber: The mix of vegetables in the bhaji makes it fiber-rich, which aids digestion.
  2. Rich in Vitamins: Contains essential vitamins like vitamin C and A from tomatoes, carrots, and peas.
  3. Antioxidants: The vegetables in Pav Bhaji offer antioxidants that help fight free radicals in the body.

Instructions to Make

  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, cauliflower)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon pav bhaji masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Butter (for cooking and serving)
    • Salt to taste
    • Pav (soft bread rolls)
  2. Instructions:
    1. Cook the Vegetables: Boil the mixed vegetables until soft, then mash them together.
    2. Sauté Onions and Tomatoes: Sauté onions, ginger-garlic paste, and cook until onions are golden. Add tomato puree and spices.
    3. Prepare Bhaji: Add mashed vegetables to the pan and mix well with the spices. Add water to get the desired consistency.
    4. Prepare Pav: Heat butter on a tawa, and toast the pav until golden brown.
    5. Serve: Serve the bhaji hot with pav, garnished with cilantro and a squeeze of lemon.
